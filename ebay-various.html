eBay Intranet Homepage

The Hub homepage is the center of the Award winning eBay intranet universe. It is the primary means for the company to communicate news and events to the many thousands of employees within eBay, and is the landing page they view each morning and throughout the day.

The Homepage communicated eBay news, events, feeds, externally shareable articles, CTA’s to high profile events.

My role in the project was

  • Designer
  • I coded up the prototype in github and collaborated with the rest of the design team to get it where we wanted it. Then handed it over to engineering.
  • Worked with engineering to ensure the integrity of the design was maintained, and oversee various changes and updates.

Leaders Week

Leaders Week brings together all of the companies top brass for a 3 day conference. In 2019 we decided to integrate Passport, the internal eBay app, to communicate and inform the attendees during the conference.

My role was Lead Designer and also Front End Developer for the m-web portions of the project.

Hub Sites

At eBay I worked on designing many of the key high visibility sites as the lead designer. These sites included the All About The Business site, which the voice of key C-Level Execs communicating to employees across the company about key financials and strategy.

I worked on the high profile launch of the eBay Diversity Site which was part of a key innitiative to launch awareness about awareness across the company. This included personally working with the Chief Diveristy Officer and his team throughout the project. The site was very well recieved as part of the inniative.

Also I worked on the redesign and relaunch of the Travel & Procurement Site which is the second most trafficked site within eBay.
