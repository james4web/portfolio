Easy Road

Get the most out of your travel
The Problem

The difficulty people have finding good vacation destinations. Travelers must sift through a great deal of information from disparate sources, which is often vague, subjective, opportunistic and/or irrelevant.

The Process

Understanding this problem included user interviews, persona's, user stories, affinity maps, competitive analysis, story boarding, userflows, card sorting, wireframes, prototypes, and more.
